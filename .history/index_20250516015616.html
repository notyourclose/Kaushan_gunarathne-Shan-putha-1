About

Kaushan Gunarathna, known as SHAN PUTHA, is a Sri Lankan rap artist, musician, and performer. With a unique style and energetic performances, he has captivated audiences across the country. His music blends traditional Sri Lankan sounds with modern rap, creating a fresh and dynamic experience for fans.
